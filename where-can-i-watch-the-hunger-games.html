Where can I watch The Hunger Games?

 Posted on April 19, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  389 words  |  Martina Birk

In 2012, the very first entry in The Hunger Games film franchise hit theaters and quickly became one of the most popular fantasy action series of our time. Based on the series of books, there was plenty more to come over the years and with each release, the dominant franchise continued to gain momentum and popularity.

The Hunger Games starring young Jennifer Lawrence, as a series boasts four films with a brand new one recently released, The Hunger Games, Catching Fire, Mockingjay Part 1, and Mockingjay Part 2 and the new film, The Ballads of Songbirds and Snakes. With all these films, there is plenty of action for new fans to dive into or for long-time fans to watch over and over again.

While the films were very successful at the box office, thanks to streaming there is no need to head out to theaters to catch the action. Instead, you can experience the entire The Hunger Games franchise back to back from the comfort of your own home.

Where Can I Watch The Hunger Games?

Right now, several platforms offer The Hunger Games movies, making streaming all four parts back to back incredibly easy. The Roku TV Channel does have the installments for free, but otherwise, you will need a subscription to watch. Peacock, Fubo TV, and Hulu have all four installments of the franchise available with a subscription.

If you don’t have one of these services, watching them will require renting or buying the films.

You can rent or buy all four The Hunger Games films from Amazon Prime, Google Play, YouTube, or Apple TV right now, and these will remain live for the foreseeable future.

The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes is currently still in theaters, but will most likely make its way for purchase to Amazon Prime Video, Vudu, and Google Play Movies.

If you’re new to the franchise here is the order that you should watch the films in.

  • The Hunger Games
  • The Hunger Games: Catching Fire
  • The Hunger Games: Mockingjay – Part 1
  • The Hunger Games: Mockingjay – Part 2
  • The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes

The Hunger Games is an iconic franchise that continues to captivate audiences worldwide. Now with the addition of streaming services, watching it is easier than ever before! Check it out on these streaming options.
