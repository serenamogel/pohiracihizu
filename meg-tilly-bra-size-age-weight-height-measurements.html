Biography:

Meg Tilly was born on February 14, 1960 in Long Beach, California, USA. She made her screen debut as a dancer in the 1980 musical drama Fame. In 1982, she made her television debut in an episode of Hill Street Blues.

She has since appeared in other shows including Road to Avonlea, Fallen Angels, Winnetka Road, Caprica and Bomb Girls. In film, she gained recognition for her roles in Psycho II, The Big Chill and Impulse.

In 1985, she played Sister Agnes in Agnes of God, for which she received critical acclaim and went on to win the Golden Globe Award for Best Supporting Actress and earned an Academy Award nomination for the same category.
