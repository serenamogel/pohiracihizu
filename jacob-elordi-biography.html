Jacob Elordi career

As a background extra in Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales, Elordi had his first experience working on a Hollywood movie set. In the 2018 Australian film Swinging Safari, he played the Rooster in his debut acting performance.

Elordi gained notoriety for his performance as Noah Flynn in the May 2018 release of the romantic comedy The Kissing Booth on Netflix.

In the follow-up The Kissing Booth 2, which was shot in Cape Town, South Africa, in the middle of 2019 and released in July 2020, he repeated the part.

In addition, he appeared in The Kissing Booth 3, the third installment of the series, which was made available on Netflix on August 11, 2021.

Elordi begins playing Nate Jacobs in the HBO television series Euphoria in 2019 after appearing in the horror movie The Mortuary Collection. Elordi made an appearance in the sensual thriller Deep Water by Adrian Lyne from 2022.

Elordi will feature in The Sweet East, directed by Sean Price Williams, and the forthcoming thriller Saltburn by Emerald Fennell. In the forthcoming movie Priscilla, he will play Elvis Presley.
