DDD-Sized Amazon Shoppers Say This Now-$16 Bra Is So Comfy

 Posted on September 1, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  425 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

It really only took one week of lockdown to turn me into the number one hater of bras. For a long time, I was able to swear off the undergarment because I wasn’t really going anywhere, which made slipping one back on all the more difficult — just one minute on and I wondered, “Was it always this uncomfortable?” Luckily, Amazon shoppers have done the hard work for us (aka, testing out bras so we don’t have to) and have reported one that’s “so comfy,” they can sleep in it. And right now, it’s on sale for just $16.

Playtex’s 18-Hour Silky Soft Smoothing Wireless Bra is available in four colors at Amazon, as well as three sets of two, which all come in sizes 36B through 48DDD. The style is designed to provide optimal support without wiring, achieved through fuller cups and higher sides. And given that I personally have a love-hate relationship with wire (love the lift, hate that it digs into my skin), I’m excited to get the same results without the all-day discomfort. 

The bra boasts more than 27,900 five-star ratings, from shoppers of varying sizes and ages. “I'm over 50 now, but I have always hated underwire bras,” wrote one who begrudgingly wore them because they “had no other options” for their 40DD chest. But then they discovered Playtex’s wireless bra, which they now wear “more than [their] other bras because it’s so much more comfortable.” “It provides really good holding power all day…and it holds up well in the laundry, too,” they claimed, deeming it “one of the most comfortable bras ever.”

Another “larger-busted” customer was equally impressed with Playtex’s style, writing that the bra “is very flattering and is extremely comfortable to wear 24 hours a day.” They were amazed by how the style is “supportive” and “very durable” (a necessity for all of us who indiscriminately throw any and everything in the washing machine), adding that “finding a bra that fits, that's comfortable, and [is] flattering is almost impossible,” but this style did all three. And according to one person with a DDD-sized chest, this wireless bra “is supportive enough to handle everything yet…might as well be…pajamas as it is so comfy.”

It’s safe to say that most of us don't enjoy wearing bras, which is why finding one you don’t mind putting on is a major win. So while Playtex’s shopper-loved 18-Hour Silky Soft Smoothing Wireless Bra is on sale for just $16, I might as well grab one (or a few).
