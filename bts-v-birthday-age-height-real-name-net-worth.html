Early Life, Education, and Parents

The popular member of the popular boy group BTS, V, was born Kim Tae-Hyung in Seo District, Daegu, South Korea. He is the eldest child of his parents, whose name is unfortunately unknown at the moment.

Likewise, he has two younger siblings, a sister, Kim Eon Jin, and a brother, Kim Jeong Gyu. V first got the inspiration to be a singer when he was in elementary school. Since then, he began taking saxophone lessons.

As for his education, V attended Korean Arts High School and graduated in 2014. As for now, V is enrolled in Global Cyber University. 

Early Career and Rise to Stardom

BTS V was becoming a trainee for Big Hit Entertainment, was a bit of pure luck. He was scouted by the company when V was accompanying his friend for an audition. The staff was utterly baffled, hearing V’s deep baritone voice as he was initially selected for his visuals.

Soon after that, he moved to Seoul to pursue his music career and began his trainee life. Even before the debut of the group, V was kept hidden as a secret member.

Likewise, he made his official debut on June 13, 2013, as a BTS member on Mnet’s M Countdown with the track “No More Dream.” 

Just like the staff, people were stunned over his deep voice, which contradicted his sweet, handsome visual. Nonetheless, V is a vocalist of the group who also contributes as a lyricist and composer.

Moreover, V co-wrote and co-produced the song ‘Hold Me Tight,’ which was included in the album ‘The Most Beautiful Moment in Life, Part 1.’ Aside from that, he is credited for songs like Fun Boyz, Stigma, Hug Me, and Winter Bears. 

In 2018, V released his second solo song, Singularity, which was a massive hit among the fandom and critics worldwide.

Different from his usual sound, the song featured a massive synth and base, which suited his raspy yet deep voice. Moreover, V was also added to the BTS dance line after the release of this song.

Following the event in October of the same year, V became one of the youngest recipients of the prestigious fifth-class Hwagwan Order of Cultural Merit medal, awarded by the President of South Korea. He received the medal alongside his group members.

Aside from singing and group activities, V is also active as an actor. In 2016, he debuted as an actor on-screen from the KBS2‘s historical drama Hwarang: The Poet Warrior Youth. He received much acclaim for his debut work.

Professional Career: BTS For Life

BTS is one of the biggest and best boy groups in the world right now. Straight from South Korea, this group consists of seven young men who share the same passion for music.

The members are Kim Namjoon, aka RM, Kim Seok-Jin, Min-Yoongi, aka Suga, Jung Hoseok, aka J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jeon Jungkook.

Likewise, the group has been together even before their debut. They made their official debut in 2013, initially as a hip-hop group, they later evolved their musical horizon and cover a wide range of genres.

Moreover, their music resonates with people of all ages as they talk about mental health, self-love and encourage people to lean towards positivity.

As of now, they have released over three Studio albums titled Dark & Wild(2014), Wings(2016), and Love Yourself: Tear(2018).
