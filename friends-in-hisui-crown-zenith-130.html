Friends in Hisui (Japanese: ヒスイの仲間たち Hisui Friends) is a Supporter card. It is part of the Crown Zenith expansion.

Card text

Draw 3 cards.
You may play only 1 Supporter card during your turn.

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card and a Full Art card in the special Crown Zenith expansion, first released in the Japanese VSTAR Universe subset. The Regular print features artwork by kirisAki, while the Secret print features artwork by Kinu Nishimura.

Gallery

Trivia

This card's effect is identical to Cheren, Tierno, Hau, Friends in Alola, Hop, Barry, Friends in Galar, Friends in Sinnoh, and Nemona.

Origin

The friends depicted are Rei, Akari, Adaman, and Irida. Mount Coronet is featured in the Regular print's illustration.
