Swan is a South Korean singer, songwriter, musician, performer, and television personality who is known as the main vocalist and maknae of the South Korean girl group PURPLE KISS. Formed by RBW in 2020, the group’s discography consists of several extended plays and tracks such as Into Violet, Hide & Seek, Cabin Fever, MemeM, Nerdy, Geekyland, My Heart Skip a Beat, Can We Talk Again, Ponzona, My My, Sweet Juice, and Zombie.

Swan Facts

  • When she was young, she lived in the Philippines for some time.
  • Swan once unsuccessfully auditioned for the JTBC survival reality show Mix Nine.
  • She has been PURPLE KISS‘ youngest member.
  • Swan was announced as the 2nd member of the girl group PURPLE KISS on July 23, 2020.
  • Her fellow PURPLE KISS groupmates include Dosie, Ireh, Yuki, Chaein, and Na Go-eun. Park Ji-eun was also a member of the group but she left on November 18, 2022, due to poor health issues.
  • Owing to mental health issues, she was on hiatus from September 30, 2020, to December 23, 2020.
  • PURPLE KISS released its debut extended play Into Violet on March 15, 2021, via RBW and Kakao Entertainment and it included songs like Can We Talk Again, Hello, Skip Skip, Period (퍼펙트), and My Heart Skip A Beat. The EP peaked at #11 on the South Korean Albums (Gaon) chart.
  • With PURPLE KISS, she released the group’s 2nd extended play Hide & Seek on September 8, 2021, through RBW and its tracklist included Zombie, Cast Pearls before swine (돼지 목에 진주 목걸이), Twinkle, So Why, 2am, and ZzZz. The EP peaked at #5 on the South Korean Albums (Gaon) chart.
  • The song Zombie from Hide & Seek was placed at number 6 in Dazed‘s list of “The Best K-Pop Tracks of 2021”.
  • On March 29, 2022, PURPLE KISS released its 3rd extended play titled MemeM and it peaked at #7 on the South Korean Albums (Gaon) chart. It consists of songs like memeM, 날좀봐 (Oh My Gosh), Pretty Psycho, Joah (좋아), Cursor, and Hate Me, Hurt Me, Love Me.
  • The track Pretty Psycho from MemeM was ranked #4 in Genius‘ “25 Best K-Pop B-Sides of 2022” list.
  • Swan was revealed as one of the 48 contestants for Mnet’s new survival show Artistock Game on September 16, 2022.
  • She has been nicknamed “mul-tto” (which means “sane weirdo”) by an RBW staff member.
  • Swan previously had a puppy and a chick.
  • One of her specialties is painting.
  • She enjoys cola drinks.
  • Apart from her mother tongue, Swan can also speak English.
