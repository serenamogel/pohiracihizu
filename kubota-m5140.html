Kubota M5140 Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota V2403-DI-T turbocharged diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled 148.5 ci [2.4 L] engine, which delivers 52 hp [38.8 kW] power at rated rpm 2600.

Kubota M5140 Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 2.4L 4-cyl diesel engine, 4×2 2WD, 4,200 lbs [1905 kg] Rear lift (at ends), 4240 to 5160 pounds weight, 8.5 qts [8.0 L] engine oil capacity, 8 forward and reverse gears.

Kubota M5140 Utility tractor attachments include Kubota LA1153 Height Position and Kubota LA1153 Power Position front-end loader.
