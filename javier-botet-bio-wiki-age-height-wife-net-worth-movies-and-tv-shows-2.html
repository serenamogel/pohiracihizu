Javier Botet Biography

Javier Botet is a Spanish horror actor who was born and brought up in Ciudad Real, Spain. He is famous for his role as Tristana Medeiros in Rec(2007) Mama in Mama(2013), Hobo in It (2017), Set in The Mummy (2017),  Slender Man in Slender Man (2018) and  Javi in Amigo (2019).
