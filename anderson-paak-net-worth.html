Anderson Paak Net Worth: $4 Million

Anderson Paak is an American musician and record producer known for his skills in singing, rapping, drumming, percussion, and keyboards. He was born in Oxnard, California in 1986. Paak released his debut studio album O.B.E. Vol. 1 under the name Breezy Lovejoy in 2012 and the album Lovejoy under the same name in 2012. He gained recognition after releasing the album Venice under the name Anderson Paak in 2014.

His album Malibu, released in 2016, was a huge success and reached #9 on the US R&B/Hip-Hop chart and #79 on the Billboard 200. In 2018, he released the album Oxnard which also received positive feedback. Paak has collaborated with several other artists including ScHoolboy Q, The Game, Macklemore & Ryan Lewis, Idris Elba, Mac Miller, Dr. Dre, Eminem, Ab-Soul, James Blake, J. Cole, and more.

Anderson Paak’s net worth is estimated to be $4 million.
